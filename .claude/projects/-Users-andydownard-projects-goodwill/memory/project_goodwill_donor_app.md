name: Goodwill Donor Receipt Webapp
description: Webapp for in-kind donors to scan QR, authenticate via phone, and create tax e-receipts
type: project
---

Building a donor contact collection and e-receipt webapp for Goodwill. Flow: QR code scan → phone auth (Twilio Verify) → create e-receipt for taxes. Shares Twilio credentials with PennyAI's Railway deployment.

**Why:** Goodwill needs a digital way to collect donor contact info and provide tax receipts for in-kind donations. Currently this is likely a paper process.

**How to apply:** Keep the app simple and mobile-first. The primary user is someone standing at a Goodwill donation center with items to drop off. Speed and simplicity matter most.
